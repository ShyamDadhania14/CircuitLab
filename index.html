Quick guide

  1. Drag parts from the left onto the board.
  2. Click a pin (the small dot), then click the pin you want to join it to. A wire appears.
  3. Pick a wire color first from the palette bar below if you want a specific color.
  4. Hit Simulate to power the board — click Arduino digital pins to toggle HIGH/LOW and watch LEDs, buzzers, motors and relays react.
  5. Export your layout as JSON any time so you can rebuild the exact same wiring on the real hardware.
